About Our Class

Class X PPLG 1 is one of the classes in SMKN 1 Tirtajaya that focuses on the software development and game development majors. As part of the vocational program, this class aims to provide quality and relevant education to the world of software industry.

PPLG

PPLG or Software and Game Development is a department at Vocational High School. This department studies and deepens the ways to develop software, ranging from creation, maintenance, quality management, and organizational management of software development.

SMKN 1 Tirtajaya

SMKN 1 Tirtajaya is a vocational high school located on Jl. Raya Pisangsambo, Pisangsambo, Kec. Tirtajaya, Karawang, West Java. The school was established in 2011/2012 and has been providing quality vocational education ever since.
